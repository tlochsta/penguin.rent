Penguin Habitats

Penguins live in a variety of habitats, from the icy shores of Antarctica to the temperate islands of the Galápagos. Learn more about where different species of penguins make their homes.

Antarctica

The icy continent of Antarctica is home to several species of penguins, including the Emperor and Adelie penguins.

Falkland Islands

The Falkland Islands provide a temperate habitat for penguins such as the Gentoo and Rockhopper penguins.

Galápagos Islands

The Galápagos Islands are home to the only penguin species found north of the equator, the Galápagos penguin.
